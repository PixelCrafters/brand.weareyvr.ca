Vancouver’s startup community hub

WeAreYVR is an information hub for the Vancouver startup community. It is a collaborative project — startup accelerators, the city of Vancouver, and members of the community are all helping to put each other on the map.

This data is important to entrepreneurs having a place to find local companies, events, and job opportunites, investors looking to connect with startups, municipalities who want to best support their local economies, and individuals looking for a new city to welcome them.
